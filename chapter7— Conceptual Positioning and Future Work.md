# Chapter 7 — Conceptual Positioning and Future Work

## 7.1 Essence of MDAD
MDAD redefines differentiation by shifting the responsibility for direction and
scale selection from the human to the OS. This creates a derivative that is
adaptive, structurally aware, and capable of avoiding singularities.

## 7.2 Position in Mathematical Structure
- OS-level differentiation  
- Built-in singularity avoidance  
- Reversal of classical numerical analysis hierarchy  
- Compatible with OS Geometry and OS-generated PDEs  

## 7.3 Future Directions
- OS-based integration  
- OS-version of Runge–Kutta methods  
- Full coupling with OS Geometry  
- Taichi-based real-time simulation
